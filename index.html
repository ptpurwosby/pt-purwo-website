About Us

Founded in 1977, PT. PURWO SURABAYA is a 4PL logistics company providing comprehensive logistics solutions, including sea freight, trucking, warehousing, and specialized cargo handling. We are trusted by both government and private sectors to deliver high-quality logistics services.

Our Services

  • Sea Freight Services (FCL and LCL) to all Indonesia
  • 20' and 40' Trailer Trucking Transportation
  • Warehousing & Inventory Management
  • Cargo Project Handling (Bulk & Over-Dimensional Cargo)
  • Logistics Consultancy & Supply Chain Solutions
  • Rail Freight Transportation

Our Fleet

Our modern fleet includes 20’ and 40’ trailer trucks, specialized cargo handling equipment, and rail transport options, ensuring seamless delivery and logistics operations.
